## Admission Requirements & Entry Pathways

| Accordion |
| --- |
| What are the English language requirements? | Lawson requires a minimum IELTS score of 6.5 (with no band below 6.0) for undergraduate programs, and 7.0 (with no band below 6.5) for most postgraduate programs. We also accept TOEFL, PTE Academic, and Cambridge C1 Advanced. |
| Can I apply directly, or do I need an agent? | You can apply directly through the Lawson University online application portal. We also work with authorised education agents in over 60 countries. A full list of approved agents is available on our International Agents page. |
| What scholarships are available for international students? | Lawson offers over 120 scholarships specifically for international students, including the Lawson Global Leaders Scholarship (full tuition), the International Excellence Award (25–50% tuition reduction), and faculty-specific merit scholarships. |
| How do I apply for a student visa? | Once you receive your Confirmation of Enrolment (CoE) from Lawson, you can apply for an Australian Student Visa (subclass 500) through the Department of Home Affairs. Our International Student Services team provides step-by-step visa guidance and can review your application. |
| What work rights do I have as an international student? | International students holding a valid Student Visa (subclass 500) can work up to 48 hours per fortnight during semester, and unlimited hours during scheduled breaks. Lawson's Career Services team can help you find on-campus and off-campus employment opportunities. |
