Lawson Centre for Climate Action

Established in 2019, the Centre for Climate Action brings together over 200 researchers from across the University to deliver integrated solutions to the climate crisis. Our flagship projects include the Southern Ocean Carbon Monitoring Program and the Urban Heat Island Mitigation Study.

Visit the Centre

Institute for AI & Society

Exploring the ethical, legal, and social implications of AI, the Institute for AI & Society is a global leader in responsible technology research. We partner with governments, industry, and civil society to shape AI policy and governance frameworks.
