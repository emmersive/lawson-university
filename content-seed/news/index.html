Research | 15 June 2026

Lawson Researchers Achieve Breakthrough in Renewable Energy Storage

A team from the Faculty of Engineering & Technology has developed a new iron-air battery technology capable of storing up to 100 hours of energy at a fraction of the cost of lithium-ion alternatives. The research, published in Nature Energy, could transform grid-scale energy storage globally.
Rankings | 10 June 2026

Lawson Climbs to #78 in QS World University Rankings

Lawson University has achieved its highest ever position in the QS World University Rankings, rising seven places to #78. The result reflects the University's sustained investment in research, teaching quality, and international partnerships.
Student Life | 5 June 2026

Lawson Students Win National Innovation Challenge

A team of five Lawson engineering and business students took home the top prize at Australia's largest student innovation competition, winning $50,000 for their AI-powered water management platform designed for regional communities.
Research | 28 May 2026

$12M Grant Awarded for Pancreatic Cancer Research

The Lawson Cancer Research Institute has been awarded a $12 million National Health and Medical Research Council grant to develop a new early-detection test for pancreatic cancer, a disease that currently has a five-year survival rate of just 12%.
Community | 20 May 2026

New First Nations Scholarship Program Launched

Lawson University has launched the Ngurangga Scholarship Program, providing 50 fully-funded undergraduate scholarships annually to First Nations students, including living allowances, mentoring, and cultural support.
Partnerships | 12 May 2026

Lawson Partners with Siemens on Smart Cities Initiative

A new $8 million joint research initiative between Lawson University and Siemens Australia will develop AI-powered infrastructure management systems for Australian cities, with pilots planned for Melbourne and Brisbane.
