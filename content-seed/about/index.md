| ## Our Story Founded in 1965 by a group of visionary academics and civic leaders, Lawson University was built on the belief that higher education should be transformative — for individuals, communities, and the world. Named after pioneering Australian author Henry Lawson, our University has grown from a single campus in Clayton to a multi-campus institution serving over 52,000 students from 140 countries. Today, Lawson is ranked among the top 80 universities globally, with a reputation for groundbreaking research, exceptional teaching, and a deep commitment to sustainability and social justice. [Read our full history](/about/our-story) | ### Fast Facts - **Founded:** 1965 - **Location:** Clayton, Melbourne, VIC - **Campuses:** 3 (Clayton, City, Caulfield) - **Students:** 52,000+ - **International students:** 18,000+ - **Staff:** 8,500+ - **Alumni:** 280,000+ - **QS Ranking:** #78 (2026) - **Research income:** $180M+ per year |
