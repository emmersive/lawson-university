Frequently Asked Questions

Accordion
When do applications open for 2027?

Applications for Semester 1, 2027 open on 1 July 2026. VTAC applications for domestic undergraduate students close 31 October 2026. Direct postgraduate and international applications close 30 November 2026.
How do I get my academic transcripts assessed?

Upload your certified transcripts through the Lawson Application Portal. International transcripts may require translation by an approved NAATI-accredited translator. Our admissions team will assess your qualifications and provide an outcome within 10 business days.
Can I visit the campus before applying?

Yes! We run campus tours daily during semester (Monday–Friday, 10am and 2pm) and hold Open Days in August each year. Book a tour through the Visit Lawson page. Virtual tours are also available online.
How do I contact a specific faculty or department?

A full directory of Lawson faculties, schools, and research centres — including direct contact details — is available on the Staff Directory page. For general faculty enquiries, email the relevant faculty office using the contacts listed on each faculty's page.
