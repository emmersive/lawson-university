## Our Campuses

| Tabs |
| --- |
| Clayton (Main) | ### Clayton Campus Our main campus in Clayton is a self-contained university city, home to most faculties, residential colleges, the Lawson Library, sporting facilities, student services, cafes, and the iconic Lawson Square. - Located 20km south-east of Melbourne CBD - Direct bus and train connections - 1,500+ acres of parkland and gardens - Lawson Medical Centre on campus |
| City Campus | ### City Campus — Melbourne CBD Our City Campus, located in the heart of Melbourne's Southbank precinct, is home to the Lawson Business School, the Faculty of Law, and our Postgraduate Hub. Immerse yourself in Melbourne's vibrant cultural and business ecosystem. - Steps from the Melbourne Arts Centre and Federation Square - State-of-the-art lecture theatres and collaboration spaces - Direct access to Melbourne's business and legal districts |
| Caulfield Campus | ### Caulfield Campus Our Caulfield Campus is home to the Faculty of Arts & Social Sciences and the Lawson Design School. A creative and collaborative environment with dedicated studio spaces, media labs, and gallery facilities. - 15 minutes from Clayton by shuttle - The Lawson Gallery hosts national and international exhibitions - Design and creative industries focus |
